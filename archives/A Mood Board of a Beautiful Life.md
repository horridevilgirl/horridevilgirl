After doing some serious soul-searching, and despite my newfound distaste for the 'collected' lifestyle, I've been reminded that I am, in fact, a visual creature. I like to be able to visualise things in a really concentrated way. Although Pinterest is useful and allows me to store images en masse, it means that I end up collecting rather than curating an idea of my idealised self. By limiting myself to four images to represent my aspiration, I become more selective, and thus more curation-al. 

When I think about the ideal version of myself (I broke this down with charts in [[Project - 6 Months to be Unrecognisable]]), I visualise someone who is in love with her natural features, grateful for the world around her, chic, interesting and intelligent. I want to love my natural hair colour, my big eyebrows, I want to wake up every day free from the compulsion to apply makeup and slick my hair down. I want to embrace my natural features, follow my natural curiosities, and just unapologetically *be.*

This mood board was curated in late March of 2026.
