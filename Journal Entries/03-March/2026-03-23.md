I've been thinking today about what it means to be gifted. I truly, truly am so lucky to occupy this body - not only because it is predominantly healthy, functional, and strong, but also because I seem to have been made to be sensitive and curious. For a really long time, I saw these as burdens. I feel everything so strongly and am so emotionally compelled, I always want to know more and learn more and nothing ever feels to be enough. I feel like I am always wasting potential. 

I found this chart, and it almost made me cry. I'm not too sure why it compelled such strong emotions for me. I don't know if I'm going to really write much more today in terms of journalling. Don't get me wrong, I am still beyond grateful for everything in my life, even the things that I perceive to be negative are gifts in their own way, whether it exists as a lesson or simply to create the conditions necessary for appreciation. But the world is very big and I am very small, and this both delights and terrifies me. There is so much to know, and I will live and die knowing so little of it. Scary. Wonderful.
